3. PERFORMANCE METRICS BY CRYPTOCURRENCY
================================================================================

METRIC DEFINITIONS:
  R² Score:         Coefficient of determination (0-1 scale)
                    Measures proportion of variance explained
                    
  MAPE (%):         Mean Absolute Percentage Error
                    Average percentage deviation from actual
                    Lower is better (0% = perfect)
                    
  MAE ($):          Mean Absolute Error
                    Average dollar-value deviation
                    Lower is better
                    
  RMSE ($):         Root Mean Square Error
                    Penalizes large errors more heavily
                    Lower is better
                    
  Dir. Acc. (%):    Directional Accuracy
                    % of correct up/down direction predictions
                    Higher is better (>50% is better than random)
                    
  Correlation:      Pearson correlation coefficient
                    Measures linear relationship (-1 to 1)
                    Higher absolute value is better (close to ±1)
